About GuestBook

Our Story

GuestBook started with a simple idea: to create a digital space where memories can be captured and preserved. Inspired by traditional guestbooks at weddings, events, and vacation homes, we wanted to bring this concept into the digital age.

Founded in 2025, our platform has quickly grown from a simple signing tool to a comprehensive memory-keeping solution used by thousands of individuals, businesses, and event organizers worldwide.

Our mission is to help people connect meaningfully, even in an increasingly digital world. We believe in the power of personal messages and shared memories to strengthen relationships and build communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every significant moment is captured not just in photos, but in the authentic words and emotions of those who experienced it.

GuestBook aims to be the global standard for digital memory preservation, offering tools that are intuitive enough for anyone to use, yet powerful enough to create meaningful digital keepsakes that last generations.

As we grow, we remain committed to privacy, accessibility, and creating technology that enhances human connection rather than replacing it.

Our Features

Digital Signatures

Collect signatures and messages from guests with our intuitive digital interface. Support for stylus input, typed messages, or even voice recordings.

Photo Integration

Allow guests to attach photos to their messages, creating a visual timeline of your event or location. Photo booth integration available.

Privacy Controls

Set your guestbook to public, private, or password-protected. You control who can view and who can sign.

Customizable Themes

Personalize your guestbook with themes that match your event, brand, or personal style. Custom backgrounds, fonts, and color schemes.

Remote Signing

Allow guests who couldn't attend in person to leave messages remotely. Perfect for global events or remembrance books.

Physical Printing

Convert your digital guestbook into a beautiful printed keepsake with our premium printing service. Hardcover and leather options available.
